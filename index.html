Hi! I'm Lucy, a front-end developer and aspiring UX/UI designer with a strong focus on crafting clean, accessible, and user-friendly web interfaces. I recently graduated from the British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT), where I majored in Front-End Web Development. I'm passionate about turning ideas into functional and visually engaging websites, and I'm always exploring new design trends and techniques to improve user experiences. Outside of coding and design, I enjoy drawing as a creative outlet—it keeps my imagination active and inspires my work in digital design.
